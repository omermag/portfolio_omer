# Scrapers

This folder contains private-use and project-based scraping systems built for real-world research, structured extraction, and automation workflows.

These scrapers were developed as part of practical systems for discovering public sources, extracting useful web data, validating entities, and feeding downstream enrichment or orchestration pipelines.

## Included Projects

### Beatport Chart Scraper – Profile Crawler
A scraper focused on crawling Beatport chart pages and related profile data for structured research and extraction workflows.

Use cases:
- Music chart research
- Profile-linked data extraction
- Structured enrichment inputs

### Official Website Crawler – Social / Contact / Form Extractor
A crawler built to scan official websites and extract relevant public-facing assets such as social profiles, contact points, and form links.

Use cases:
- Official site validation
- Social and contact extraction
- Form discovery
- Website-based enrichment support

### Deep Google Research URLs Actor
A research scraper designed to discover and organize relevant URLs from deeper search flows for validation, review, and enrichment processes.

Use cases:
- URL discovery and expansion
- Research-source collection
- Pipeline input generation
- Structured source mapping

## How These Scrapers Are Used

These projects are typically used inside broader automation systems such as:
- lead research pipelines
- enrichment workflows
- validation systems
- n8n-based orchestrations
- table-driven data pipelines
- agent-assisted research processes

## Scope

Most of the projects in this folder were created for:
